Layathmboori

Beginner

A structured course designed for students aged 8 and above to acquire foundational basics of Kannada Light Music. The curriculum features in-depth training in pitch control, rhythm mastery, and thorough learning of a new song every four classes.

Learning Objectives:

  • Voice & Pitch Control: Developing control over vocal expressions through an adapted form of 'Sa Pa Sa' practice from Carnatic classical music.
  • Rhythm Mastery: Introduction of Gere Guruthina Thaala, a teaching technique developed exclusively to internalise our natural rhythm and connect it to that of music.
  • Kai Thaala Integration: Developing practical execution of hand beats to aid in timing improvisations (Paluku, Swara Kalpane, Akaara) with the tala cycle.

Fee Structure:

One-time Admission Fee: ₹3000 (Non-refundable)

Recurring Fee: Monthly ₹1200 / Quarterly: ₹3500 (To be decided)

Raagathamboori

Intermediate

A structured course designed for students aged 8 and above to learn and master Kannada Light Music. The curriculum features in-depth training in voice modulation, Kai Thaala perfection, vocal expression and thorough learning of a new song every four classes.

Learning Objectives:

  • Voice & Pitch Control: Developing control over vocal expressions through an adapted form of 'Sa Pa Sa' practice from Carnatic classical music.
  • Kai Thaala Integration: Developing practical execution of hand beats to aid in timing improvisations (Paluku, Swara Kalpane, Akaara) with the tala cycle.
  • Expression & Emotion in Singing: Understanding lyrical depth and developing the modulation to effectively convey its meaning as a vocal expression while singing.
  • Group & Solo Performance Skills: Enhancing confidence and stage presence needed to perform seamlessly in group settings with coordination and adaptability, while also strengthening their skills as solo performers.
