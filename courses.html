Experience the Rich Heritage of Kannada Light Music

Join our specialized programs designed to cultivate musical excellence in students of all ages

Learning Outcomes:

  1. Enhanced Musical Discipline: Develop a structured approach to music practice and performance.
  2. Individual Corrections & Feedback: Personalized training sessions to refine skills.
  3. Masterclass Involvement: Opportunity to be part of special in-person workshops by Dr. Sunitha Chandrakumar and other top artists.
  4. Active Participation in Sankeerthane: Perform in live devotional singing events.
  5. Online & Offline Concert Opportunities: Showcase learning through periodic events.

Schedule:

  1. One live class per week, totaling four classes per month.
  2. Classes available on Wednesday, Friday, or Saturday.
  3. Initial three classes led by instructors, followed by a fourth class by Dr. Sunitha Chandrakumar.
  4. One assignment to be submitted for feedback every month.

Content:

Light music genres covering:

  1. Bhavageethe
  2. Devaranaama
  3. Janapadageethe
  4. Tatvapada
  5. Vachanas

Reference Materials:

  1. Lyric sheets for easy learning.
  2. Audio & Video material with synchronized lyrics.
  3. Reference video demonstrating song presentation.

Guidelines:

  1. Minimum attendance requirement of 75% to progress to the next learning stage.
  2. Classes held via Google Meet, and Google Classroom to be used for upload of reference materials, classwork, and assignment submissions.
  3. Recordings will not be shared to ensure prioritisation of class attendance. Topics missed will be repeated in future classes to recap.
  4. Attending other batches would not be possible, as we aim to maintain a maximum number of students per session.

Layathmboori

Beginner

A structured course designed for students aged 8 and above to acquire foundational basics of Kannada Light Music. The curriculum features in-depth training in pitch control, rhythm mastery, and thorough learning of a new song every four classes.

Learning Objectives:

  • Voice & Pitch Control: Developing control over vocal expressions through an adapted form of 'Sa Pa Sa' practice from Carnatic classical music.
  • Rhythm Mastery: Introduction of Gere Guruthina Thaala, a teaching technique developed exclusively to internalise our natural rhythm and connect it to that of music.
  • Kai Thaala Integration: Developing practical execution of hand beats to aid in timing improvisations (Paluku, Swara Kalpane, Akaara) with the tala cycle.

Fee Structure:

Recurring Fee: Monthly ₹1200 / Quarterly: ₹3500 (To be decided)

Raagathamboori

Intermediate

A structured course designed for students aged 8 and above to learn and master Kannada Light Music. The curriculum features in-depth training in voice modulation, Kai Thaala perfection, vocal expression and thorough learning of a new song every four classes.

Learning Objectives:

  • Voice & Pitch Control: Developing control over vocal expressions through an adapted form of 'Sa Pa Sa' practice from Carnatic classical music.
  • Kai Thaala Integration: Developing practical execution of hand beats to aid in timing improvisations (Paluku, Swara Kalpane, Akaara) with the tala cycle.
  • Expression & Emotion in Singing: Understanding lyrical depth and developing the modulation to effectively convey its meaning as a vocal expression while singing.
  • Group & Solo Performance Skills: Enhancing confidence and stage presence needed to perform seamlessly in group settings with coordination and adaptability, while also strengthening their skills as solo performers.
