Student Recital

Classical Piano and Violin Performances
Music Classical Live

About the Event

Join us for an enchanting evening of classical music performed by our talented students. This event showcases the remarkable progress of our piano and violin students as they perform a carefully curated selection of classical pieces.

The program includes works by Mozart, Beethoven, Bach, and Tchaikovsky, providing a diverse range of classical masterpieces for your enjoyment. Students range from beginners to advanced levels, demonstrating the growth and development of their musical abilities.

Refreshments will be served during the intermission, providing an opportunity for guests to socialize and discuss the performances.

Summer Music Camp

Intensive Week-long Music Program
Music Workshop Children

About the Event

Our Summer Music Camp is a week-long intensive program designed for young musicians eager to develop their skills and collaborate with peers. The camp offers a comprehensive musical experience with expert instruction, ensemble practice, and performance opportunities.

Students will participate in daily lessons, group activities, and specialized workshops covering various aspects of music theory, technique, and performance. The week culminates in a showcase concert where students demonstrate their accomplishments.

The camp is suitable for children aged 8-16 with at least one year of prior musical experience. All instruments are welcome, with specialized tracks available for piano, violin, guitar, vocals, and percussion.

Faculty Concert

An Evening of Musical Excellence
Music Concert Classical Contemporary

About the Event

Experience an extraordinary evening of musical artistry featuring our acclaimed faculty members. This special concert showcases the exceptional talent of our instructors, who are accomplished musicians in their own right.

The program spans a wide range of musical styles, from classical masterpieces to contemporary compositions, demonstrating the versatility and expertise of our faculty. The concert will feature solo performances, duets, and ensemble pieces, providing a rich and diverse musical experience.

This is a rare opportunity to witness these talented musicians perform together in a professional concert setting. The evening will conclude with a reception where attendees can meet the performers and discuss the music in a relaxed atmosphere.
