Step into a vibrant world where tradition meets passion. Based in Mysuru and with a rich history of over two decades, our school is dedicated to preserving and nurturing the rich heritage of Indian light music—from soulful devotional melodies to the dynamic rhythms of Sankeerthane.

Set with the vision of
Dr. Sunitha Chandrakumar
Raghuleela School of Music was established on February 1, 2003 in Mysuru. Dr. Chandrakumar, nurtured in a family steeped in musical tradition and trained in classical music as well as dance from an early age, was inspired by her esteemed gurus, Sri S. G. Raghuram and Smt. H. R. Leelavathi. She founded the school to honor their teachings and to create an institute that preserves the subtle nuances of Indian light music (and thus the name penned with admiration, 'Raghuleela')
Beginning with a modest focus on devotional and folk tunes—including the distinctive rhythms of Nagara Sankeerthane—the school has evolved to offer musical teaching that seamlessly blends traditional techniques with contemporary musical expressions.

Vocal Excellence Journey

Our comprehensive training approach integrates traditional Carnatic techniques with modern pedagogical methods to nurture well-rounded musicians.

🎵

Voice & Pitch Control

Master the foundations of vocal expression through our adapted 'Sa Pa Sa' practice methodology derived from classical Carnatic traditions. Develop precise pitch control, seamless transitions between notes, and the ability to maintain shruti alignment.

Breathing Techniques Shruti Alignment
🥁

Rhythm Mastery

Experience our exclusive Gere Guruthina Thaala teaching methodology that bridges the gap between your internal rhythm and musical structure. Develop a profound understanding of tala cycles and the ability to maintain perfect timing during complex passages.

Tala Structures Rhythmic Precision
✋

Kai Thaala Integration

Transform your understanding of rhythm through the embodied practice of Kai Thaala. Learn to synchronize hand patterns with vocal expressions, enabling seamless improvisation within tala frameworks including advanced techniques for Paluku and Swara Kalpane.

Hand Gestures Improvisational Freedom
🎭

Expression & Emotion

Delve into the emotional heart of Carnatic music through our comprehensive approach to Bhava. Develop the nuanced vocal modulation necessary to convey the depth of lyrical meaning, learning to embody the sentiment behind each composition with authentic expression.

Lyrical Interpretation Emotional Conveyance
🎤

Performance Mastery

Cultivate the confidence and presence of a seasoned performer through our dual approach to solo and ensemble singing. Develop seamless coordination with accompanying artists, stage presence, and the adaptability required for both intimate and grand performance settings.

Stage Presence Ensemble Coordination
